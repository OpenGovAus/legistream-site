Info

Legistream is a modern and simple way to watch Australian parliaments live. It was created with the goal of centralising the parliaments to a single area, without the need to use the often outdated and slow parliament websites. Legistream was developed by and is actively maintained by OpenGov Australia, an independent team of developers based in Australia with the common goal of making it easier for the general public to securely access political information relevant to them over the internet.
Legistream does this by optimising the parliamentary streaming experience, isolating the streams to one parent website, all natively within the userâ€™s browser. It also bypasses the need to use a Flash player on both the Queensland and Northern Territory streams, offering a more secure experience.

OpenGov Australia hopes you find the service useful!
